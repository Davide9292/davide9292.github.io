Hi! I'm Davide Pedone, a hybrid Product Designer.

I bring a diverse skill set in product design, covering physical interfaces, e-commerce, design systems, CRO, hi-fidelity prototyping, motion design, apps, CMF, IoT, and more.

I graduated with honors in Communication Design from Politecnico di Milano, laying the foundation for my career.

I then kickstarted my journey specializing in HMI at Habits Studio, contributing to the innovation of physical interfaces.

Shifting gears, I transitioned into the digital domain, working with ICT and DBT agencies.

Currently, I hold the position of Senior Product Designer at Publicis Sapient, where I collaborate closely with Service Designers, Strategists, CRM Specialitsts and Software Engineers within the agency to ensure cohesive and impactful solutions.

Over the past 7 years, I've successfully delivered projects for notable clients such as Stellantis, Panasonic, WindTre, Schindler, Haier, Carrefour, Valentino, Esa, Pirelli, among others.
