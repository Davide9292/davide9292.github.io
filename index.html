Hi! I am Davide Pedone, a hybrid Product Designer.

At the moment, I am immersed in the task force dedicated to defining Publicis Groupe's AI vision. Our ongoing efforts involve not only conceptualizing but also actively developing concrete solutions to empower and elevate the group's capabilities on an international scale.

I graduated with honors in Communication Design from Politecnico di Milano.

Later on, I kickstarted my journey specializing in HMI at Habits Studio, contributing to the innovation of physical/digital interfaces.

Shifting gears, I transitioned into the digital domain, working with ICT and DBT agencies.

Currently, I hold the position of Senior Product Designer at Publicis Sapient, where I collaborate closely with Service Designers, Strategists, CRM Specialitsts and Software Engineers within the agency to ensure cohesive and impactful solutions.

Over the past 7 years, I've successfully delivered projects for notable clients such as Stellantis, Panasonic, WindTre, Schindler, Haier, Carrefour, Valentino, Esa, Pirelli, among others.
