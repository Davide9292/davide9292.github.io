Davide Pedone

Hybrid Product Designer

Hybrid by experience, I bring a diverse skill set in product design, covering physical interfaces, e-commerce, design systems, CRO, hi-fidelity prototyping, motion design, apps, CMF, IoT, and more.

Educationally, I graduated with honors in Communication Design from Politecnico di Milano, laying the foundation for my career.

I then kickstarted my journey specializing in HMI at Habits Studio, contributing to the innovation of physical interfaces. Shifting gears, I transitioned into the digital domain, working with ICT and DBT agencies.

Currently, I hold the position of Senior Product Designer at Publicis Sapient.

Over the past 7 years, I've successfully delivered projects for notable clients such as Stellantis, Panasonic, WindTre, Schindler, Haier, Carrefour, Valentino, Esa, Pirelli, and others.
Later on, I focused on the purely digital aspects of the discipline, working in ICT and DBT agencies.
